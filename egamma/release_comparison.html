Comparison of Rel19 / Rel20

When tuning an Alignment robust offline menu we noticed a difference in efficiency between Rel19 and Rel20 for offline (similar to those reported on May 4th here).

Tuning is done with Rel19 samples and some validation is done on Rel 20.

Rel19, Rel20 Nominal LH Menus

The problem seems to mostly be at high Eta.

  • Pileup for rel 19 and rel 20 are different, but not enough to fully explain the diferences.

  • We have done some comparisons on the variables using N-1 LH, Bit-by-Bit efficiencies and looking at the variable distributions themselves.

Summary

  • In tuning new menus we noticed a difference in offline efficiencies between Rel 19 and Rel 20.
  • It looks like several variables are contributing, but a little more work needs to be done to disentangle any pileup effects.
  • This difference seens to come mostly from high Eta.
  • Will talk to Calo and tracking experts to find out exactly what changes were made going into Rel 20 and if these were intensional.
